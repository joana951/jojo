Quizzes

First of, know the teacher. By knowing the teacher you can like basically master what to review and how to review that certain subject.

Ask. Ask the other sections, simple. 

Sit in the front. Since mostly classrooms there have platforms, the teacher's blind spot is actually in front because they can actually see the students better at the back.

Never pass early. The classroom becomes chaotic at the near end of the quiz, making asking questions less visible to the teacher.

Petas

This is the most weighted among the three. You can focus in these more to assure higher grades.

Do your best. Only when the peta is individual. 'Cause some 'pabibo' will set the standard and you must top that.

Support. Sometimes you can literally do nothing for contribution and you'll still have a grade. Just be there when you're needed, do not spend that much effort.

Again, do your best. Do this if the Peta requires group member ratings. Or just be nice.
